Register for Sports in 4 Steps

Use this like a pre-game checklist. Complete the paperwork first, then students can be cleared for tryouts, practices, and games.

Coach's reminder

Sports physicals should be uploaded to Register My Athlete. Please do not turn physicals in to teachers, office admin, or athletic directors.

1First down

Schedule Physical

All K-12 athletes must have a sports physical on file and uploaded to Register My Athlete to participate in tryouts, practices, and games.

Upload to RMA only
2Set the roster

Register My Athlete

Create or update your Register My Athlete account before tryouts. Families may be prompted to pay a $25 fee for tryouts and participation.

  • K-5: choose Elementary grade bracket.
  • 6-8: choose Middle School.
  • 9-12: no label, just the sport.
3Verify ID

National Sports ID

Elementary and middle school students complete National Sports ID. Families may need a birth certificate or passport, Infinite Campus screenshot, and clear student photo.

K-8 only
4Finish strong

Pay Sports Fee

Most elementary and middle school athletes have a $150 team fee per athlete per season. Pay through Infinite Campus or at the front office only if the student makes the team.
