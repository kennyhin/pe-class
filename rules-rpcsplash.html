04

RPC Splash

All Grades · K–5
Objective: Win the hand battle to stay dry. A classic Rock-Paper-Scissors with a high-stakes water twist.
💦 Game Rules
1
Face Off
Line up 1-on-1 with an opponent across from you.
2
The Shoot
Perform a standard "Rock, Paper, Scissors" shoot on the count of three. Both players reveal at the same time.
3
The Splash
The winner gets to splash the loser using a sponge, cup, or spray. Losers take the splash with good sportsmanship!
4
Keep it Moving
After each round, rotate to a new opponent. Keep the energy up and the line moving.
