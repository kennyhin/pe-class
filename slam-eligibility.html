Athlete clearance board
Stay eligible to play

Athletics are a privilege. Students must be present, academically accountable, and in good standing to participate in practices, tryouts, games, races, and contests.

Game day rule

Attend school that day

Students must attend school on the day of an athletic practice or competition. A student who misses school may not participate in practice, tryouts, games, or races that same day.

Appointments

Check in or out

For medical appointments or prearranged absences, students must check in or out through the front office. Any partial or full-day absence needs an excused parent note on file.

Red flag

Chronic absence

Students who are chronically absent are ineligible for athletics. Attendance matters before the season and during the season.

Practice plan

Practice attendance

Consistent practice attendance is part of game eligibility. Each head coach sets the team practice attendance policy and will work with students involved in other school activities when possible.

F
Academic accountability

Carry-over suspension policy

A scholar-athlete who has an F in a core subject across two consecutive eligibility grade checks may receive a one-game suspension. If there are no games left that season, the suspension carries over to the next sport and must be served in the first eligible game.

Final call

Athletic director decision

Students are expected to represent SLAM! with positive behavior, academic effort, and teamwork. Final eligibility and discipline decisions rest with the athletic director.
