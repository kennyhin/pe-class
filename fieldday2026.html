What to Bring

Student Checklist

🧢
Hat
Sun protection is key. Any brimmed hat works.
💧
Water Bottle
Filled and labeled. Nevada heat is no joke.
🏖️
Towel

Sunscreen
Applied by parents before school. Outdoors all day.

Proper Shoes
Athletic shoes only. No flip flops or sandals.

Class Color
Wear your grade's color. Dress code still applies.

Obstacle Course
