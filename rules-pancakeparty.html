03

Pancake Party

All Grades · K–5 · Kinder Playground
Objective: Work as a team of "chefs" to move as many pancakes (poly spots) as possible onto your team's plate (hula hoop).
🥞 Game Rules
1
The Chef's Tool
Every player uses a frying pan (folder or clipboard) to carry their pancake (poly spot). No hands on the pancake directly.
2
One at a Time
Teams may only move one pancake at a time. You cannot start a second pancake until the first is on the plate.
3
One Foot Frozen
When you are holding a pancake on your pan, you must keep one foot "frozen" to the floor. You cannot walk or run while carrying it.
4
The Hand-Off
Gently slide the pancake from your pan to a teammate's pan. As soon as you pass it, you are "unfrozen" and can move to the end of the line.
5
The "Oops" Rule
If a pancake touches the floor or any part of your body, it is "ruined" and must return to the starting bucket to begin again.
6
Scoring
Successfully slide the pancake onto the hula hoop "plate" to score a point for your team.
